Our Process

Home About Us Our Process

We are optimized to always deliver the best in class web work that will fulfill all requirements to turn your business into a success. Experiences from our previous ventures has helped us to develop a deliberate and successful methodology to build astounding sites. This proven process has significantly changed our approach to the development process for the better. Thanks to our methodology, we can now process multiple projects at a time at faster turnaround rate. These essential techniques gives us a strong guideline for your website development process, enabling us to exceed your expectations. We separate each venture down to these stages:

Getting Idea

In WordSphere every venture starts with the “Request A quote” form. We will require detailed information about your requirements. You can send us any reference that you want to see in your new site. If you already have a site, you can send us the link along with the changes you desire to have.

Getting Idea

Proposal

We will analyze your project immediately after getting your request. Our experienced project planning team will study every small detail to give you a reasonable estimation. When we are done with our evaluation, we will send out project proposal with detailed process and expenditure.

Proposal

Getting Start

Once you accept our proposal, we will start a project contract. As we receive the initial funding along with the signed contract, the journey of a new venture starts in WordSphere..

Getting Start

Project co-ordination

In this step we design the wireframe of all the pages needed for your site. This is simply a visual approach to perceive how all the pages of your site are intertwined and cooperating with each other.We'll work with you to accumulate your site contents that will eventually populate your new site. Our QM group will start monitoring the advancement of your project through Synchronous Quality Assurance System (SQAS) from this step.

Project co-ordination

Prototyping

We will analyze your project immediately after getting your request. Our experienced project planning team will study every small detail to give you a reasonable estimation. When we are done with our evaluation, we will send out project proposal with detailed process and expenditure.

Prototyping

Coding

Once the visual designing has been concluded and endorsed, we will cut it up and the coding stage will initiate. Right now you can only sit back while we dive into the coding and programming of your site.WordSphere developers are experienced in making your concept into a 100% working WordPress site which meets all the highest standards. Using the latest techniques and future-proof coding, we will make a fully responsive site with cross browser compatibility.

Coding

Test & Review

Finishing the coding of your site and our Quality Monitoring Group give the clearance now is the ideal time for the site to be tried by you. You will check each and every small part of the website and give us your recommendations.After your final test run and modifications, it is just a matter of time to launch your brand new site.

Test & Review

Launch

Finally, this is the moment we all will be waiting for. After finishing the final optimizations on the design and testing the codes, your site is ready to launch. Another successful WordSphere venture is ready to make its voyage in the ocean of WordPress.
